SafePlate Analyzer

Food safety at the speed of science

  • Tell Me More

Safeplate Analyzer is a portable mass analyzer
designed for inspectors to test for food allergens.

Safeplate Analyzer is a portable triple-quadrupole mass spectrometer designed for rapid detection
of allergenic proteins in restaurant foods. Inspectors can analyze samples on-site in under 20 minutes,
screening for over 100 known allergen proteins including nuts, gluten, dairy, soy, and shellfish.

FAST

The mass spectrometer can scan food samples for all allergens in just 20 minutes! The handheld version can be pre-programmed for a specific subset of allergens to get results in minutes

CONVINIENT

The inspector version is the size of a laptop making it portable and easily carried from location to location. The consumer version is even smaller being the size of a cellphone!

SAFE

The SafePlate Analyzer features a triple quadrupole so allergens can be identified and distinguished from other proteins in the food very easily
