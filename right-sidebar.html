You may be wondering "How do I use this product?"

There are many uses depending on what your specific intentions are

The SafePlate Analyzer

The product is portable so it is easy to carry from place to place. Simply enter the sample in the compartment on the side and wait 10-20 minutes for the sample to be analyzed for any allergens

The SafePlate Analyzer is a handy tool for inspectors due to it's speed and specificity. The mass spectrometer features a triple quadrupole to easily decifer between allergens with similar masses.

The inspector version

This version is meant for the inspector. It is the size of a laptop and can easily be carried from one place to another.

The handheld version

This version is meant for the average consumer with severe allergies. The device can be pre-programmed to detect for only your allergens so the test can be done in minutes
